Lasagna

Description

Layering flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce (bolognese/ragu) or creamy b√©chamel, a ricotta/cheese mixture, and mozzarella, then baking until bubbly and golden brown for a comforting, hearty baked dish.

Key steps involve simmering the sauce, mixing cheeses with egg, assembling layers, and baking until hot and melted, with resting before serving.

Ingridients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two can of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
